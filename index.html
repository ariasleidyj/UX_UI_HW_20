    Background

    How we started

    Our team was given three weeks to brainstorm, design, and execute a solution aimed at solving a real human problem that will help improve our daily lives.

    The Problem

    The DC Metro system has long been outdated and frustrating to use. Currently, there is no way to manage your Metro information through a mobile device. And now, due to the recent outbreak of COVID-19, residents of the DMV area are even more cautious, reluctant, and anxious about their future use of the DC Metro.

    The Solution

    We believe introducing a way for Metro riders to have easy access to their SmarTripÂ® information and accurate Metro rail/bus times will provide more contactless options and allow them to quickly, efficiently, and safely get to their destination.

    User Pain Points

    1. Having to go online or us fare vending machine to check SmarTrip card balance
    2. Stressing about having to add value to card before getting on the train
    3. Metro website can be confusing and not user friendly
    4. Lack of service breakdown communication
    5. General Safety

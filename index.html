    Background

    How we started

    Our team was given three weeks to brainstorm, design, and execute a solution aimed at solving a real human problem that will help improve our daily lives.

    The Problem

    The DC Metro system has long been outdated and frustrating to use. Currently, there is no way to manage your Metro information through a mobile device. And now, due to the recent outbreak of COVID-19, residents of the DMV area are even more cautious, reluctant, and anxious about their future use of the DC Metro.

    The Solution

    We believe introducing a way for Metro riders to have easy access to their SmarTrip® information and accurate Metro rail/bus times will provide more contactless options and allow them to quickly, efficiently, and safely get to their destination.


    Research

    Metro Riders' Pain Points

    1. Having to go online or us fare vending machine to check SmarTrip card balance
    2. Stressing about having to add value to card before getting on the train
    3. Metro website can be confusing and not user friendly
    4. Lack of service breakdown communication
    5. General Safety

    Wireframes and Usibility Testing

    Final Thoughts and Lessons Learned

    1. Management of SmartBenefits® account 2. Live train/bus tracking (rider can see exact location of train/bus) 3. Include metro discounts on app, allowing riders to get discounts for their loyalty to DC Metro 4. Allow Metro riders to use cellular device instead of SmarTrip card as their pass on metro trains and buses

    It is essential to stick to the plan set out in the beginning. Often times it can be tempting to get ahead of ourselves and think about all the features we want instead of talking to users. It’s important to not let our biases influence our design process.

    Never assume your plan is finite or set in stone. We went in thinking that we could focus on one aspect of the product, but after talking with our users, we quickly realized that we were neglecting half the problem by doing so. Being flexible and ready for changes allowed us to adapt our plan and build a much better product than initially imagined.
